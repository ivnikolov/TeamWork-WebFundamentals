At the end we have an exam and everybody who didn't take it will die painfully (We just kidding. We actually don't know how you'll gonna die but we assume it will be painful). Ofcourse this level is ABSOLUTELY free of charge but in the end you have to pay 50 Galactic Credit Standarts (GCS). But it's ABSOLUTELY FREE !!!

He took the exam of Level 0 with full points and currently working for the Imperial task force (wich is some kind of "no one cares" job). He will give you semi adequately understanding of what you need to do to pass the exam.
