Level 1 - Force Basics

In this level you will learn what is like to be a Jedi programmer. You will learn basic skills in : what is a lightboard, how you can program with it, which are the best programming languages and IDEs for your lightboards, and most inportantly - how to use the force of code to defeat dark side programmers! The final exam is a test of strength with the code, and everyone who didn't pass will have to apply again. The code force is weak in all those who failed. Training in this level is absolutely free of charge, except the fee of 50 gallactic credits for the exam. But otherwise, it's absolutely free!
